Rules

    1. Program Eligibility

      The Rewards Program is open to registered and qualified participants who work for Rewards Program. Void where prohibited by law. Participation in the Program is free. Any other related Program benefits are not transferable and may not be combined or merged among other participant's accounts. Any acquired companies, will no longer be eligible for participating within the program. Participant must have activated their account in Rewards Program before earning points. All acquired companies will have 30 days to utilize the balance of their points.

    2. Program Dates

      The rewards program will begin accruing for activity as of the month of the year in that you sign up. Management reserves the right to terminate this program at any time, without notice. Upon termination of the program, Participants will have 30 days after the end of the program to redeem points for merchandise, events and travel.

    3. Earning Points

      Reward Points will be awarded as follows:

      • One point for every dollar(s) spent from January-December 2019 with participating suppliers.
      • A flat payout of 45,000 points by surpassing 10% growth from January-December in 2019, over the same period in 2018, with participating suppliers

      More promotions and perks will be announced throughout the program.

    4. Statements

      Participants will receive paperless electronic rewards monthly. Point statements may also be viewed online at the program web site via the My Account page.

    5. Point Expiration

      All Reward Points issued to a Participant will expire after 12 months of no earning activity through a promotion. Any Reward Points shall be forfeited without compensation.

    6. Non-Transferrable/No Cash Value

      Points are non-transferable and are not redeemable for cash.

    7. Availability

      All rewards of merchandise, ticketed events, airline and hotel accommodations, and any other travel included in the Rewards Program are available only to Program Participants who have received Points as part of this loyalty points-based program. Reward items offered are subject to availability and may be changed at any time.

    8. Rewards

      Please refer to the online reward offering for the most up-to-date details on Rewards, Point values, and Redemption information. Eligible Participants may redeem Points only for items listed in the online reward offering at the designated point quantities.

    9. Questions

      All questions should be sent using the contact form located within the program web site.

    10. Participants

      Rewards Program reserves the right to inactivate participants who are not in good standing. Rewards Program reserves the right to disqualify participants from eligibility for rewards and revoke any rewards issued.
